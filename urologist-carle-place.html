About Dr. Kashani

Dr. Mahyar Kashani is a urologist and men's health specialist serving Long Island and Nassau County. At this Carle Place office — centrally located on Old Country Road — he sees patients from Westbury, Garden City, Mineola, Hicksville, and surrounding communities, treating erectile dysfunction, low testosterone, penile implants, Peyronie's disease, and a full range of men's urological conditions.

Services at Our Carle Place Office

Dr. Kashani offers the full spectrum of men's health and urology services at this location.

Erectile Dysfunction

Oral medications, injection therapy, vacuum devices, and surgical options tailored to your diagnosis.

Penile Implants (IPP)

Inflatable penile prosthesis for men who have not responded to other ED treatments.

Low Testosterone (TRT)

Comprehensive testosterone evaluation and replacement therapy — injections, gels, or pellets.

Peyronie's Disease

Xiaflex injections, traction therapy, and surgical correction for penile curvature.

Premature Ejaculation

Evidence-based pharmacologic and behavioral treatment options.

Vasectomy

No-scalpel vasectomy performed in-office with minimal downtime.

Vasectomy Reversal

Microsurgical vasovasostomy and vasoepididymostomy for men seeking to restore fertility.

Enlarged Prostate (BPH)

Medical management and minimally invasive procedures for urinary symptoms from BPH.

Kidney Stones

Diagnosis, medical expulsion therapy, and referral for lithotripsy or surgical intervention.

Overactive Bladder

Behavioral modification, medication, and advanced therapies for OAB.

Circumcision

Adult circumcision performed in an outpatient setting.

Hydrocele

Evaluation and surgical repair of scrotal fluid collections.
