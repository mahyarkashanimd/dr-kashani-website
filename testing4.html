Mahyar Kashani, MD

Director of Male Sexual Health

Dr. Mahyar Kashani is a fellowship-trained urologist and Director of Male Sexual Health at Men's Health Long Island, serving patients across Long Island and New York. He focuses on the diagnosis and treatment of Erectile Dysfunction, Low Testosterone, Peyronie's Disease, and Male Performance Concerns.

Dr. Kashani provides comprehensive, physician-led care—offering everything from medical therapy to advanced procedural treatments, including penile injections and implants. His approach is discreet, evidence-based, and tailored to each patient's goals.

"We're here to help you get back to feeling like yourself again—strong, confident, and in control."
