Research & Publications

Peer-Reviewed Academic Contributions | Dr. Mahyar Kashani, MD

Dr. Kashani has co-authored over 20 peer-reviewed publications in journals including the Journal of Urology, European Urology, European Urology Focus, and Continence. His academic work spans men's health, sexual medicine, renal oncology, and urologic surgery — the foundation of his evidence-based approach to patient care.

Dr. Mahyar Kashani, Director of Male Sexual Health, provides expert urological care across Long Island and Nassau County.
