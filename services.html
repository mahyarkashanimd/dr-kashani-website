Clinical Services

Advanced medical and surgical solutions tailored to your needs.

Erectile Dysfunction (ED)

We offer the full spectrum of ED treatments, moving from conservative management to advanced surgical restoration. Dr. Kashani is a specialist in restoring function when other treatments have failed.

Penile Doppler Ultrasound

Diagnostic testing to evaluate blood flow and determine the root cause of ED.

Oral Therapies

Optimization of PDE5 inhibitors (Viagra, Cialis) and customized dosing protocols.

Penile Injection Therapy

Custom-compounded intracavernosal injections (ICI) for reliable, robust erections.

Penile Implants (IPP)

The gold standard for permanent ED resolution. Dr. Kashani specializes in the Inflatable Penile Prosthesis.

Men's Sexual Health & Fertility

Addressing structural and functional concerns that impact intimacy and confidence.

Peyronie's Disease

Treatment for penile curvature and plaque, including Xiaflex injections and surgical straightening.

Premature Ejaculation

Behavioral and medical therapies to improve control and sexual longevity.

Male Infertility

Evaluation and treatment of male factor infertility to help couples conceive.

Vasectomy

Minimally invasive, safe, and effective permanent contraception for men.

Testosterone Replacement Therapy (TRT)

Safe, monitored hormone optimization for men suffering from low testosterone.

Hormone Evaluation

Comprehensive blood panels to accurately diagnose low testosterone and exclude other causes.

Customized TRT

Injection, gel, or oral therapies tailored to maintain physiologic levels and improve energy, libido, and mood.

Low Libido Management

Addressing the hormonal and psychological factors contributing to low sex drive.

General Urology

Expert management of common and complex urinary tract conditions.

Kidney Stones

Medical management and minimally invasive surgical removal (Laser Lithotripsy) of stones.

Enlarged Prostate (BPH)

Treatments for urinary frequency and difficulty, including medication and minimally invasive procedures.

Hydroceles

Surgical repair of fluid accumulation around the testicle to relieve discomfort and swelling.

Circumcisions & Phimosis

Surgical management for tight foreskin (phimosis) or cosmetic circumcision.

Elevated PSA

Thorough evaluation and screening for prostate health and prostate cancer risk.

Hematuria

Comprehensive workup (cystoscopy and imaging) to evaluate the cause of blood in the urine.
