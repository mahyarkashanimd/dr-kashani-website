Mahyar Kashani, MD

Director of Male Sexual Health

Dr. Mahyar Kashani is a board-eligible urologist and specialist in men’s sexual health, serving patients across Long Island and New York. As Director of Male Sexual Health and Assistant Professor of Urology at Mount Sinai South Nassau, he focuses on the diagnosis and treatment of erectile dysfunction, low testosterone, Peyronie’s disease, and male performance concerns.

Dr. Kashani provides comprehensive, physician-led care—offering everything from medical therapy to advanced procedural treatments, including penile injections and implants. His approach is discreet, evidence-based, and tailored to each patient’s goals.
